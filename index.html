Why this version works

  • Starts with one strong state instead of an overloaded national demo.
  • Shows how a donor can move from state view to city view to school view.
  • Makes impact feel more concrete with city checkpoints and school breakdowns.
  • Can later expand to other states using the same interaction model.
Suggested pitch: "This map lets judges see Compudopt's footprint at three levels: state, city, and school."
