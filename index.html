Case competition concept

A national impact map that makes Compudopt's local work feel visible, human, and scalable.

This proposal imagines a donor-friendly web experience where visitors hover over a state, see the cities and schools Compudopt has supported, and instantly understand the story behind the numbers. All school and impact data in this demo is fictional and included only for presentation purposes.

Why this concept works

  • Turns a national footprint into something interactive for judges, donors, and partners.
  • Combines emotional storytelling with credibility through school-level and city-level metrics.
  • Supports both hover exploration on desktop and click interaction on mobile devices.
  • Can later connect to real data from program operations, annual reports, or partner districts.
Suggested pitch: "Compudopt is already changing outcomes across communities. This map helps people see that impact, state by state."
